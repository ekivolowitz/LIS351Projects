Peer Review

It may come as a shock, but no one reviewed my project. So, I had some of my roommates and friends review my project for me.

What my friends said

  1. Use $ sign formatting with commas after every three digits on the price page.
  2. Have hovering on table rows to help navigate the large tables in my project.
  3. Hyperlink the objects that are searchable in my little database.
  4. Shorten the sender and receiver addresses in various tables.
  5. Have a way for the user to make their own queries.

Responses to Their Feedback

I think that all the suggestions my friends made were great points. All of them except for the querying component were pretty easy to implement, which was a big plus. For querying, I tried two different ways. The first is in the /address/* field where I DO NOT use SQL and simply match based on text and shorten the table. I found the filtering feature on w3schools and really liked how simple and fast it was. The next form I did, I created the ability for anyone to make their own SQL queries. I hope that this makes up for not having tons of hardcoded queries. That was my favorite part of the project as I found it the most difficult.
